Guatemalan biologist and entomologist, currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the [University of Kansas Biodiversity Institute](https://biodiversity.ku.edu/people/samanta-orellana). As part of the [Symbiota team](https://symbiota.org/about-us/) and the [GBIF Network](https://www.gbif.org/mentors), actively collaborates with Latin American collections to advance their biodiversity data initiatives.
